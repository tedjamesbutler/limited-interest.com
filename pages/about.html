Limited Interest 2014-2017

Small run experimental / electronic tape releases, dubbed in-house on chrome cassettes. Curated by Alessandro Cortini (SKARN, SONOIO, Nine Inch Nails) and Ted Butler. Strictly limited editions.

Five releases were issued between 2014 and 2017, each limited to 50 copies on chrome cassettes with hand-assembled packaging.

Los Angeles, CA.
